Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 29, 2025
Protecting the data of the global aviation community.

At PilotWardrobe, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our marketplace, mobile apps, and pilot-specific services.

Quick Summary: We collect data to help you buy, sell, and swap aviation gear. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers. We only share information necessary to facilitate transactions or improve your experience.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked to you ("Personal Data").

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and pilot certificate type (optional) to verify your community status.
  • Listing Data: Photos, descriptions, and pricing for the aviation gear you post.
  • Verification Data: If you apply for a "Verified Pilot" badge, we may collect professional credentials or ID.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we use your location to show gear available at nearby hangars or FBOs.
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, and how you interact with our marketplace.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate secure transactions between buyers and sellers.
  • To verify the authenticity of pilot-specific equipment listings.
  • To provide AI-powered listing descriptions and gear recommendations.
  • To comply with aviation-specific safety or trade regulations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information in these limited circumstances:

  • With Other Users: When you buy or sell gear, we share necessary details (like shipping address or preferred FBO pickup location) with the other party.
  • Service Providers: We use third parties for payment processing (e.g., Stripe), cloud hosting, and AI services.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect the safety of the aviation community.

4. Your Privacy Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access & Correction: You can update your profile and listings at any time through your dashboard.
  • Data Deletion: You may request account deletion. Once processed, your personal data is removed, though some transaction records may be retained for legal/tax purposes.
  • Marketing: You can opt-out of promotional emails via the "Unsubscribe" link.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transfers. While no system is 100% secure, we take significant measures to protect your gear listings and financial data from unauthorized access.
